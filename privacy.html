1. Introduction & General Overview

We, at 24Dismil.com ("Company", "we", "us", "our") and our affiliated companies, are deeply committed to respecting your online privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information ("Personal Data") you share with us. This comprehensive Privacy Policy (“Policy”) governs our website, mobile applications, and sub-domains available under the 24Dismil.com ecosystem (collectively, the “Platform”).

This Policy describes in exhaustive detail how Info Edge India Limited (in this context operating as 24Dismil.com) collects, uses, processes, discloses and transfers personal data of users while browsing the Platform or availing specific real estate services, advertising services, or other products therein (the “Services”).

By providing your explicit consent to this Policy, either directly on the Platform by checking a box, clicking "I Agree", or through other means, or by simply accessing the Platform and Services, you unconditionally consent to the Company’s processing of your Personal Data in accordance with this Policy. 2. Exhaustive List of Personal Data We Collect

We systematically collect various typologies of Personal Data about you when you access our Platform or Services. This data collection spans multiple dimensions to ensure seamless service delivery:

A. Information you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Personal Identifying Details: This includes your full legal name, permanent and temporary addresses, primary and secondary e-mail addresses, primary mobile phone numbers, landline numbers, and login credentials including usernames and heavily hashed passwords.
  • Comprehensive Property Details: If you are an owner, builder, or dealer listing a property, this includes exhaustive details of the property: nature of the property (commercial, residential, agricultural, industrial), pinpoint location details (GPS coordinates, plot numbers, survey numbers), property profile (carpet area, built-up area, super built-up area, dimensions, road connectivity, facing direction), high-resolution photographs, videos, floor plans, pricing details, tax details, and an exhaustive list of amenities provided.
  • Strict Identification Documents (KYC): Information that helps us identify and verify your legal identity or your property’s legal standing. This includes scanned copies or numbers of government IDs (such as Aadhaar, Voter ID, Driver’s License, PAN Card, Passport) and property documents (utility bills, sales deeds, property registration documents, occupancy certificates, property tax receipts).
  • Financial & Payment Interactions: We utilize PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment service providers (like Razorpay, Stripe, or BillDesk) to process your payments for premium listings or subscriptions. You will be re-directed to a third-party interface. While we do not store core financial data like raw credit card numbers or UPI PINs on our servers, we do collect and store transactional metadata, invoice numbers, payment status, subscription tiers, and billing addresses.
  • Extensive Communication Records: Absolute records of all your interactions with our customer success teams. This includes audio recordings of support calls (for quality and training), chat logs, emails, SMS records, physical mail correspondence, feedback forms, survey responses, and dispute resolution artifacts.

B. Information we collect automatically when you use our Platforms:

  • Granular Usage Data: Deep behavioral data collected when you access the Platform. This includes your specific search queries, filters applied (price range, BHKs, localities), properties shortlisted, properties viewed, times at which you access the Platforms, duration of sessions, heatmap data on where you click, and complex inferences of your real estate intent based on algorithmic analysis.
  • Hardware & Technical Specifications: Data about the precise devices, software, and technology you use. This covers hardware models, operating system versions, IP addresses (both IPv4 and IPv6), preferred languages, unique device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address), advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID), network data (carrier, connection type), and granular web browser details.
  • Location Tracking Data: Both general location data (city, region, country inferred from IP) and precise GPS location data (if you grant the mobile app permission to access your device's location services to show properties "near me").
  • Comprehensive Cookie & Beacon Data: We use an array of cookies (session, persistent, strictly necessary, performance, functional, and targeting cookies) and similar technologies (web beacons, clear GIFs, pixel tags, log files) to recognize your device across sessions, remember your preferences, and track your interaction with our email campaigns.

C. Information we receive from federated third-party sources:

We regularly augment our datasets with information from external sources. This includes data from advertising networks, marketing partners, data brokers, and public government registries (like RERA portals). Furthermore, when you use a Single Sign-On (SSO) provider (like Google, Facebook, or Apple) to log in to our Platform, we receive securely transmitted data including your authenticated name, verified email, public profile picture, and access tokens.

While we consider your privacy paramount, the nature of a real estate marketplace inherently requires the sharing of data. We may disclose Personal Data under the following exhaustive scenarios:

  • With the Open Marketplace (Other Users): When you post a property or express interest in one, your core contact details (Name, Phone Number, Email) and property details become accessible to the counter-party (buyers, renters, owners, brokers) to facilitate the transaction.
  • With specialized Service Providers & Vendors: The Company aggressively utilizes third-party infrastructure. We share data (overriding NDNC registration where applicable) with cloud hosting providers (e.g., AWS, GCP), SMS gateways (e.g., Twilio, Gupshup), email service providers (e.g., SendGrid), payment processors, KYC verification agencies, customer support software (e.g., Zendesk), and field verification agents who physically visit properties.
  • Banking and Financial Partners: Based on your explicit expression of interest regarding home loans or financing, we will share your deep financial and personal profile with participating banks, Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs), and their immediate agents so they may contact you directly.
  • Corporate Restructuring & Mergers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, corporate re-organization, amalgamation, bankruptcy, or sale of substantial assets, your enormous trove of Personal Data will be transferred to the acquiring entity as a core business asset.
  • Law Enforcement & Government Authorities: We will share personal data without hesitation when legally mandated by subpoenas, court orders, warrants, or explicit requests by local, state, or federal law enforcement to assist in investigations regarding cybercrime, fraud, or national security.

The security and absolute confidentiality of your personal data is a top corporate priority. We have invested massive capital into enterprise-grade security architectures. This includes AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 encryption in transit, strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) for our employees, mandatory Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for administrative access, regular third-party penetration testing, and continuous vulnerability scanning of our web architecture.

Despite our exhaustive safeguards, no transmission of data over the public Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. You provide your Personal Data to the Platform at your own absolute risk.
