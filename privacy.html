Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction & General Statement

We, at 24Dismil.com and our affiliated companies, are committed to respecting your online privacy and recognize your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information you share with us. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) governs our website available at 24Dismil.com and describes how we collect, use, disclose and transfer personal data of users while browsing the Platform or availing specific services therein.

By providing your consent to this Policy, either on the Platform or through other means, or accessing the Platform and Services, you consent to our processing of your Personal Data in accordance with this Policy.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following types of Personal Data about you when you access our Platform or Services:

A. Information you give us:

  • Personal Details: Name, contact information (addresses, e-mail addresses, phone number), and login information.
  • Property Details: Nature of the property, location details, property profile, photographs of your property, pricing details, and amenities.
  • Identification Documents: Information that helps us identify and verify you or your property (such as utility bills, sales deed, property registration documents, etc.).
  • Payment Information: We use third-party payment service providers. We do not store and do not have access to your financial information such as bank account numbers or credit/debit card numbers.
  • Communications Details: Records of your calls, emails, messages, or your communications in other forms related to feedback, issue reports, or customer support.

B. Information we collect when you use our Platforms:

  • Usage Data: Data about how you access the Platform, such as search queries, time spent on pages, and intent capture.
  • Technical Data: Information about the devices, software, IP address, preferred languages, network data, and web browser details.
  • Cookies Data: We use cookies or other similar technologies to recognise your device, remember you, and support the continuity of your experience.

C. Information we receive from other sources:

We may receive information about you from third parties, such as advertising and marketing partners, or when you use a third-party account (like Google) to log in to the Platform.

3. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your Personal Data to provide the Services and access to the Platform. This includes:

  • For account creation, onboarding, and registration purposes.
  • For listing your properties, extending property listings, or recording your interest in particular properties.
  • For establishing connectivity with other members of the Platform (such as buyers, sellers, or dealers).
  • Marketing Activities: To provide you with marketing and promotional material, if you have consented to receive such communication.
  • Platform Improvement: To improve our Platform and its content, provide new or better features, and personalize your experience.
  • Fraud Prevention: For identifying suspicious users, ensuring network security, and preventing/investigating crimes or fraud on the Platform.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with any legal obligations and establish, exercise, or defend legal rights.

4. Who We Share Your Personal Data With

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it under certain circumstances:

  • With other Platform Users: To facilitate real estate connections and inquiries between buyers, renters, owners, and dealers.
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors, consultants, and service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf (e.g., hosting, payment processing, analytics).
  • Legal & Safety: We may disclose information to respond to subpoenas, court orders, legal process, law enforcement requests, legal claims, or government inquiries, and to protect and defend the rights, interests, health, safety, and security of 24Dismil.com, our users, or the public.

5. Storage and Protection of Personal Data

We use robust, industry-standard security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. For user authentication, we utilize Google Authentication (OAuth) via Firebase. We do not store your Google password.

6. Retention of Personal Data

We retain your Personal Data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, reporting, or business requirements. When we no longer have an ongoing legitimate business need to process your personal data, we will either delete or anonymize it.

7. User Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You can usually manage your information within your account settings or by contacting our administration team.

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites, apps, and services. We do not control these third-party platforms and are not responsible for their privacy statements. When you leave our Platform, we encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit.
