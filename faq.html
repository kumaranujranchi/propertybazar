Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about listing, searching and transacting on 24Dismil.

Click Post Property in the header, sign in or register, and follow the step-by-step form to add property details, photos and contact information. Posting basic listings is free.
We perform basic verification checks on listings. For certain properties a verified badge is applied after additional checks. Always arrange in-person visits before finalising any transaction.
Basic listings are free. We offer paid promotional packages for higher visibility. See the Pricing page for current plans.
Log in, go to My Listings, select the listing and choose Edit or Delete. If you cannot access your listing, contact support via the Contact page.
Each listing shows contact details or a contact form. Use the contact button on the property page to message the seller or post a query.
The verified badge indicates additional checks were performed on the listing or the owner. It helps buyers trust the authenticity, but is not a substitute for due diligence.
Always verify ownership documents, visit the property in person, check local RERA registration (where applicable), and avoid advance payments without proper verification.
Use the Report button on any listing or reach out via the Contact page with details; our team will review and take action.
